Move the map, then scrub through the day

The marker updates latitude and longitude directly. The levers below drive the live snapshot without reloading the page.

How to use it
  1. Start with Melbourne, Jakarta, or Boston, then adjust the map only if needed.
  2. Choose the window facing direction and size your room and window.
  3. Use the two levers for day of year and time of day.
  4. Watch the compass, elevation gauge, and room snapshot update live.

Try Melbourne, north-facing window, June 21, around 12:00 for a clear winter floor patch.
